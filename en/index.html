FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What you need to know before starting.

How much does a website cost?

The investment depends on scope, number of pages, content and integrations. After discovery, you receive a proposal suited to the project.

How long does it take?

The timeline depends on scope and approval speed. The proposal presents the stages and an estimate before work begins.

Does STANDLOUD write the content?

We can structure the message, guide the content and develop the copy included in scope. Technical materials depend on client collaboration.

Will the website work on mobile?

Yes. Mobile is designed as its own experience and validated across different screen sizes.

Are domain and hosting included?

We can advise on and configure these services. Recurring domain, hosting or platform costs are presented separately.

Is maintenance available?

Yes. Maintenance, improvements and post-launch support can be contracted according to need.

How does payment work?

Terms are defined in the proposal according to scope and schedule, before the project begins.

Does the project belong to the client?

After completion and agreed payment, the client receives the files and access credentials specified in the contract.
