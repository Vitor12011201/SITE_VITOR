FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Before we begin.

How much does a website cost?

The price depends on the size, functionality and complexity of the project. After understanding your needs, we provide a custom proposal.

How long does it take?

The timeline depends on the scope. A simple landing page may be completed faster, while larger websites require more stages.

Will the website work on mobile devices?

Yes. Every project is designed to work across desktop, tablet and mobile devices.

Do you provide maintenance?

Yes. We can handle updates, adjustments, improvements and post-launch support.

Can I request changes?

Yes. The process includes review and approval stages.
